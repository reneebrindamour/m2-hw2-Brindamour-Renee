About Me

My name is Renee Brindamour. I was born, raised, and currently live in Ann Arbor, Michigan. I have a 16-year-old son, a Shih Tzu named Hudson, and two ornery cats named Sonic and Tails. My son named the cats after characters from one of his video games.

What brought me to the University of Florida to pursue my Master's degree?

I lost my dad to stage 4 lung cancer in January 2024. He was my best friend. Before he passed away, he made me promise that, in his legacy, I would pursue happiness, always strive to do what was best for my son and me, and love life. I decided at that moment to continue my education in his name. So, here I am. My son was my reason for getting my BA, and my dad is my reason for pursuing my MA.
